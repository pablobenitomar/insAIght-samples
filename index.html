English

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the capability of machines and computational systems to perform tasks that traditionally required human intelligence. This includes learning, problem-solving, pattern recognition, natural language processing, and decision-making. These systems use algorithms and mathematical models to process data and generate results while adapting to new information.
